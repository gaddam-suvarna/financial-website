Why Identification Cards Matter

Understanding the importance of PAN, Aadhaar, Voter ID, and more in your daily life and financial identity.

Aadhaar Card

Used for identity verification, subsidy benefits, and linking with mobile and bank accounts.

PAN Card

Mandatory for tax filing, large financial transactions, and opening a bank or trading account.

Voter ID

Essential for voting in elections and often used as a proof of identity and residence.

Driving License

Permits driving vehicles and serves as both an ID and address proof for various purposes.

Passport

Used for international travel and also serves as a high-level identity verification document.

Ration Card

Provides access to subsidized food under government schemes and is used as proof of address.

Health Insurance Card

Required during hospitalization to claim benefits under government or private health schemes.

Student ID

Used to access student benefits, scholarships, and institutional identity verification.
